How to Download & Install Microsoft 365 or Office 2021

Follow these steps to download and install Microsoft 365 or Office 2021 on your PC or Mac.

Step 1: Sign in to download Microsoft 365

Go to Office.com and select "Sign in" if you're not already signed in. Sign in with your Microsoft account.

Step 2: Select Install Office

Once signed in, select "Install Office" from the Office.com homepage, then choose "Office 365 apps" to start the download.

Step 3: Install Microsoft 365 or Office 2021

Open the downloaded file from your browser's downloads list and select "Yes" when asked to allow the app to make changes to your device.

  • Windows: The installation will start automatically.
  • Mac: Double-click the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.

Step 4: Activation

After installation, open any Office app (Word, Excel, etc.) and accept the license agreement. Microsoft 365 or Office 2021 will be activated automatically.

Need further help?

Visit Microsoft Support for more troubleshooting steps and assistance with installation issues.
